Zomato — Complaint & Refund Resolution Case Study

Scraped 30K Zomato reviews and classified 3,462 complaint reviews into 10 themes using DeBERTa NLP (zero-shot, multi-label, threshold 0.70, TOP_K=2). Found via co-occurrence analysis that support failure and refund delays almost always appear in the same review — they share a root cause, not just a surface pattern. Multi-category spikes on Mar 8–9 and Mar 17–18 confirmed systemic operational failures. Translated everything into a BRD with 9 Must Have requirements and 5 stakeholder-mapped user stories.

Model calibration decisions
MODEL SELECTION
DeBERTa (zero-shot) via Hugging Face
Chosen for zero-shot capability — no labelled training data needed. Validated on test phrases before full run: 90–96% confidence, correct classification.
THRESHOLD CALIBRATION
0.90 → 0.80 → 0.75 → 0.70 ✓
0.90 forced single-category only. Iterated to find balance between precision and dual-category coverage. 0.70 gave the best signal-to-noise on complaint reviews.
TOP-K CONSTRAINT
Top-K = 2 per review
Each review assigned to at most 2 categories above threshold. Prevents over-labelling while capturing genuine multi-theme complaints like support + refund.
